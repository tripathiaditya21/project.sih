Healing Hands is a platform that connects patients with local doctors and clinics for easy appointment bookings.
To book an appointment, simply search for a doctor by specialty, choose a doctor, and confirm the booking online.
You can manage appointments through your dashboard, where you can reschedule or cancel appointments.
If you face any issues, please contact support via live chat or email us at support@healinghands.com.
We accept various payment methods including credit card, debit card, and online wallets.
Use the search filters to find doctors by specialty, location, reviews, and experience.
